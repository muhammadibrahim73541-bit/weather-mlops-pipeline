**English Poem:**

In three cities, far and wide,
Aalborg, Doha, and Lahore reside.
Their climates differ, each unique and true,
Aalborg's chill, Doha's heat, Lahore's warmth shines through.

Aalborg's breeze whispers through the night,
A gentle coolness that soothes the sight.
But Lahore's summers are a scorching flame,
While Doha's temperatures burn with fervent name.

Rain falls not in these three cities' might,
Aalborg's dryness, Doha and Lahore's dry plight.
But which one's the best? Let's weigh the scores,
Aalborg's coolness, Lahore's warmth, Doha's burning cores.

Lahore wins this round, its warmth so divine,
A blend of comfort and climate that's truly sublime.
The Mughal charm, the food, and the night,
Make Lahore the winner in this three-city fight.
