**English Poem:**

Across the globe, three cities shine,
Lahore, Doha, and Aalborg in line.
Their temperatures reveal a tale so fine,
Aalborg's 9.4, Lahore's 28.5, Doha's 27.6.

The winds of change, a factor to consider,
18.0 km/h in Aalborg, 21.0 in Doha, 8.0 in Lahore, ponder.
Rainfall, a sign of heaven's mercy true,
Aalborg's dry, Lahore's and Doha's, a story anew.

As a traveler, which city would you choose?
Lahore's warmth, Doha's charm, or Aalborg's gentle muse.
For a winter's escape, Lahore's sun-kissed delight,
Or Doha's vibrant night, might be the one in sight.
But for a calm retreat, Aalborg's peaceful shore,
A perfect haven, for those seeking more.

So if you're looking for a city to adore,
Choose Lahore's golden light, or Doha's mystic score.
But if you yearn for a tranquil, peaceful nest,
Aalborg's gentle breeze, will be the one you'll rest.
