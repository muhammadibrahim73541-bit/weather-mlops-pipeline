**English Poem:**

In distant lands, three cities lie,
Aalborg, Doha, Lahore, a traveler's sigh.
Each has its charm, its own unique tone,
A tale to tell, a story to be known.

Aalborg's wind whispers secrets cold,
9°C in winter, a chill to hold.
But Doha's warmth beckons, a sunny delight,
31.2°C, a heat that shines so bright.

Lahore's breeze carries scents of old,
29.1°C, a temperature to behold.
No rain in Lahore, a dry desert air,
But Doha's downpour floods with abandon fair.

So where to go, a traveler's quest?
Aalborg's cool, Doha's fire is the best.
But Lahore's gentle heat, a comfortable stay,
Makes it the winner, in this weather day.

Doha might be hot, but its spirit is grand,
Lahore's gentle warmth, a soothing land.
Aalborg's chill, a refreshing breeze,
But Lahore's beauty, a sight to please.

So pack your bags, and off you go,
To Lahore, where the heart finds its home, don't you know.
